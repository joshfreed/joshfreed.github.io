LOOK Assessment

The LOOK Assessment provides licensed clinicians important information for evaluations and treatment case planning about the sexual interests/attractions of individuals who have committed a sex crime.

The LOOK Assessment multifaceted data results provide useful clinical/evaluative information both about healthy and non-healthy sexual interest. This information is critical to better understand ongoing risk assessment, diagnosis and case planning.
