Xoodle

Xoodle is an app intended to help business owners think strategically about their business. My client wanted an iOS version of a flash card product he prints. The flash cards display strategic questions to help the reader grow their business.

The app is written in Swift. There's back end to integrate with but it uses Core Data to store its data locally.

Sadly, this app was removed from the app store when the client decided not to renew his Apple Developer account.
