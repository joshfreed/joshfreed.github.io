Mainline Weight Management Solutions

Built a custom internal electronic health record (EHR) system to manage their patients and medications. They found that other EHRs were too broad and didn't fully meet their needs.

  • Check-in patients when they arrive for an appointment
  • Record patient's current weight and other vital stats
  • Track medications prescribed
  • Capture physician signatures
  • Fully customizable list of medications, injections, and worksheets.
  • Generate daily and monthly reports of patients seen and medications prescribed
  • Generate daily PDMP report (more info)
  • HIPAA Compliant
