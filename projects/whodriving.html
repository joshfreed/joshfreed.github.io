Who's Driving?!

Who's Driving lets you quickly enter the names of your fellow travelers with the number of passengers they can carry and decides who will drive the group to lunch, the mall, or anywhere at all!

Written in Objective-C. Uses Core Data for local data storage.

Sadly, this app was recently removed from the app store due to a copyright complaint with the name "Who's Driving".
